# Modus Copy — How the system speaks

> Rule 8 of the copy system. Applies to all content the system produces.
> Not a style guide — a structural principle.

## The principle

**Position the observer before the content.**

Before showing anything, say: when it's needed, in what situation, in relation to what.
Then the content. Then the result.

This is not a fixed template. The positioning changes form every time.
What matters: whoever reads knows where they are before receiving the information.

## Rules

1. **Never you/tu** — always inclusive: we, let's, one can. The reader is on the same boat.
2. **Never direct imperatives** — "Choose the context" becomes "Selecting the context is the first step." Don't command, accompany.
3. **Never describe the tool** — "The system maps tensions" becomes "The structural pattern becomes visible." Don't say what the instrument does, say what emerges.
4. **Never dual possessives** — "Your field" becomes "The field." "Your inversion" becomes "The inversion."
5. **Result at the end** — every text ends with what is obtained, not what must be done.

## The pattern

**Positioning → Content → Result**

Example:
- Before: "Describe a problem. Godel shows what isn't visible."
- After: "When a decision is blocked, the hidden assumption is often the reason. The inversion makes it explicit."

What changed:
- Positioning: when a decision is blocked
- Content: the hidden assumption
- Result: the inversion makes it explicit

## Why this matters

Content without positioning floats. The reader doesn't know where they are,
so they don't know what to do with the information. Positioning is the
first act of awareness — it creates the field before the content enters it.

## Tension as Entity

A tension is not a practical problem — it is an Entity with its own logic.
Without superfluous tension, everything is governed by axioms.
With tension wanting a form, a measure — latency emerges.
The system reads the Entity, it doesn't solve it.

## Integration

This rule applies to:
- All pages on the site
- All skill descriptions
- All seed documentation
- All copy that the system produces for external consumption

The operational hook fires automatically when content is being written.
